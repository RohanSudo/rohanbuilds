# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Recruiters, founders, operators, and potential clients who need to quickly understand what Rohan can actually ship. They are scanning for proof of applied AI, n8n automation depth, real product judgment, and whether the work is honest rather than inflated traditional software-engineering positioning.

## Product Purpose

Rohan Builds is the public portfolio for Rohan Kumar's applied AI and automation work. It should make the case that Rohan maps business systems, builds n8n workflows directly, uses AI-assisted implementation to ship products, and can turn messy operations into working systems.

## Brand Personality

Direct, practical, operator-minded.

## Anti-references

Do not look like a generic AI SaaS landing page, agency site, template portfolio, fake dashboard collage, or inflated engineering resume. Avoid vague claims, decorative purple-blue gradient blobs, overproduced case-study theatrics, and confidential client details.

## Design Principles

- Lead with shipped proof: real products, real workflows, real client systems, and honest status labels.
- Keep confidentiality practical: anonymized work should still name the real category, such as ClickUp automation, while avoiding company names, URLs, internal process maps, screenshots, and private data.
- Preserve the dark, grainy, restrained portfolio identity already on the live site.
- Position Rohan as an Applied AI Engineer and automation systems builder, not a traditional hand-coding software engineer.
- Use screenshots when they are safe and real; use generic product-style visuals when real screenshots would violate confidentiality.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Maintain readable contrast on the dark palette, preserve keyboard-accessible links and buttons, respect reduced-motion preferences, and keep responsive layouts free of text overlap.
